Week 1

Monday 11th November

Introduction to the module. On this day we learnt more about what to do for each assignment and how the format of the lessons was going to work from then on. We looked online for inspiration for our new website which was very helpful and during the photography lesson we learnt more about cameras and how to work with them.

I will apply the inspiration that I got from looking at other live websites onto my own, making sure that I still use my own ideas for it. I will also use the skills that I learnt during the photography lesson throughout this module as one of the assignments includes being familiar to cameras and taking 5 pictures which, I then will post on my website.

 

Tuesday 12th November

During the first part of the morning we created our own GitHub and Atom accounts and learnt how to use them alongside each other in order to make our website. We pushed our first versions of our websites to the internet and made the first changes to the themes we chose. During the photography lesson we learnt things such as rule of thirds, fill the frame and other techniques.

Using Atom and GitHub at the same time is a skill that will be of extreme importance throughout the module as this is what allows us to make changes to it and making them live. Different photography techniques are what will allow us to take different and interesting photos which will give us better grades when we submit our portfolios. 

 

Wednesday 13th November

This digital Wednesday’s activity was to read the Dieter Rams Design Principles presentation, choose one of the principles, find two apps/websites that demonstrate the principle and why, share your link, findings and thoughts in a group Teams conversation.

This activity gave us further inspiration for our website which we will use in the future.

 

Thursday 14th November

On this day we learnt more about linking pages within the website and how to edit the navigation bar which made a huge difference to the website as it all is now coming together. As well as this, during photography lesson we learnt more about different genres which was inspiring for when we take our own pictures for the assignment.

Linking pages is essential in website building. Without it, the website would be incomplete so by learning this I built a skill that I will most certainly use in the future as I develop my website further.

 

Friday 15th November

Today I was absent due to illness but during class everyone learnt more about changing fonts on their websites and how to change colours. There was no time for photography on this day due to a TTC presentation.

Changing fonts and colours will allow me to personalise my website further and really make it look the way I want it to.

 

Week 2

 

Monday 18th November

Today during the morning we made more changes to our website with the help of Christine who looked at our work individually and taught us how to put extra content on our website that we wanted to put in it. On the second part of the morning we  looked at a website from Canon which taught us more about aperture, depth of field, light, among other things.

Canon’s website gives a better idea of what the difference between the camera settings are. We got to change the settings and automatically find out how each change the picture in their own way.

 

Tuesday 19th November

Today we learnt how to insert images onto our website using unsplash’s free photos. I chose all black and white pictures which really fit with the theme of my website. During the second part of the lesson we watched a video which taught us more about the difference between RAW and JPEG images and briefly learnt more about photo editing on photoshop.

Inserting images changes the website completely as it allows me to develop the theme that I was going for which is everything in black and white. This will be very helpful in the future when I need to upload my own pictures and videos to the website.

 

Wednesday 20th November

Today’s digital Wednesday activity included reading three guides which talked about dealing with files and HTML and CSS basics. After that we had to write on the collaborative glossary and work on our design log.

This activity gave us a better idea of how to work with HTML and CSS which is essential for future website building.

 

Thursday 21st November

Today I made essential changes to my website which included changing fonts to all lowercase and change the accent colours of the website which is now a deep burgundy. During the second part of the morning we went into town to take pictures for our portfolio.

I was able to change things that I did not do last week due to my absence. This allowed me to personalise my website further.

 

Friday 22nd November

Today we spent the morning finishing our prototype website and our design logs.

Finishing the prototype website and design logs in time for the deadline will allow me to get a good grade.

Today we learnt more about colour theory and how It affects the mood of people looking at the website and how colours can tell the viewer what the brand represents. For example: purple=royalty. We also did an activity in groups which required us to name things that made us think of a certain colour. During the last part of the lesson we learnt about capturing and editing audio as well as where to keep sound levels when to record and how to set up microphones. This was very useful because we will need these skills to complete the assignment for this module.

Colour theory will be implemented on my website and my pictures as I really want both to represent certain qualities. Audio recording is a skill that I learnt about previously but today I got to improve on it which will be very useful in the future.

 

Tuesday 26th November

Today we learnt about what HTML and CSS is which is very important for website building and designing. The lecture was very interesting to me as this is something that was very confusing to me prior to today. After our break we learnt more about how to interview people and how to make the interview interesting for the viewer. This was very important as we will need to conduct an interview for the audio part of our assignment, and I want to make mine as interesting and grasping as I can.

Learning about interview techniques is a skill that I will need in the future so furthering this skill is essential. I have conducted interviews in the past but I feel more prepared to do so as of today.

Chrsitmas Markets

For this picture I decided to focus on the lights coming from the top of the stalls in the christmas markets. The buildings surrounding the lights and happiness of the markets are all in black and white which promotes the creepy feeling that I was trying to go for with my pictures. It obeys the rule of thirds as I used a grid to achieve this photo.

Building

This is my favourite picture out of all the photos I took due to its symetry and editing which was achieved by using a set of different tools on photoshop.

Lights in the streets

In this picture I highlighted the lights of the stalls coming from the christmas markets again.There is a branch on top which completes the picture as it fills in a gap on top. I intended for this picture to look dark and eerie despite the lights coming from the bottom of the picture.

Stars

This was the first picture I took for the project. It set the tone for the theme of my photos for this module. I did not use a grid for this picture because I wanted it to not look straight to make it more interesting.
